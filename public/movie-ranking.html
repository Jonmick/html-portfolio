My Favorite Movies

It's my top 3

Spiderman

Bitten by a genetically modified spider, a shy and clumsy high school student gains incredible abilities as an arachnid. He will soon understand that his goal is to use them to fight evil and defend his neighbors.

Bloodsport

Frank Dux's dream is to participate in Kumite, a secret and illegal life or death kickboxing tournament. But to do this he must first defeat the ruthless Ghong Li, the current champion.

Shrek

A long time ago, in a distant swamp, there lived an ogre named Shrek. One day, his precious solitude is interrupted by a bunch of fairy tale characters invading his house. They were all banished from their kingdom by the evil Lord Farquaad. Determined to give them back their kingdom and regain the solitude of their swamp, Shrek makes a deal with Lord Farquaad and goes to rescue Princess Fiona, the king's future wife. However, the princess hides a dark secret.
